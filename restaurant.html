Marvellous March

This month's featured restaurant brings us to Ethos at Oxford Circus!

You'll be spoilt for choice with Ethos' array of vegetarian delights, including their infamous veggie scotch eggs, deep fried halloumi and potato lyonnaise. There's a brilliant selection of wholesome salads, hearty dishes and elaborate desserts to choose from. Just pile up your plate with whatever you fancy and take it over to the counter to be weighed. Perfect for meeting special dietary requirements and catching up with and old friend after work. I'd definitely recommend saving some space for dessert!
